NextLevel Gaming

Website Planning Document (WDD231)

Site Name

NextLevel Gaming

The name “NextLevel Gaming” represents progression and improvement within gaming culture. It suggests helping players discover better games and enhance their gaming experience. The name is short, memorable, and clearly connected to video games.

Optional Domain: nextlevelgaminghub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the NextLevel Gaming website is to provide gamers with news, reviews, and recommendations about video games across multiple platforms including PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo. The website helps users discover new games, learn about trending titles, and decide which games are worth playing or purchasing.

  • Game reviews
  • Gaming news updates
  • Recommended games lists
  • Beginner-friendly gaming suggestions

Scenarios

  • What new video games are currently popular or trending?
  • Which games are best for beginners who are new to gaming?
  • Is a specific game worth buying before I spend money on it?
  • What platform should I use to play a certain game?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Background (#121212): Main page background to reduce eye strain.
  • Dark Purple (#2C2C54): Header, navigation areas, and sections.
  • Neon Blue (#00CFFF): Accents, headings, and highlights.
  • Light Gray (#EAEAEA): Body text for readability.

This dark gaming-style palette creates a modern appearance while maintaining strong contrast and accessibility.

Typography

  • Orbitron: Used for headings and titles to create a futuristic gaming look.
  • Roboto: Used for paragraphs and body text for readability.
